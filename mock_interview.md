  <h2>Reflection on Mock Interview Experience (STAR Method)</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Situation:</strong> As part of the work readiness training for the ICT curriculum, I needed to record a mock interview video to simulate the expectations and pressures of a real technical job interview.</li>
    <li><strong>Task:</strong> I had to concisely answer typical interview questions, highlight my technical and soft skills, and present myself professionally within a strict 2-minute time limit.</li>
    <li><strong>Action:</strong> I thoroughly researched common behavioral and technical interview queries, prepared structured responses leveraging my past experiences (such as my role as a Team Lead), set up a clean background, dressed appropriately, and refined my delivery through multiple recording takes.</li>
    <li><strong>Result:</strong> I produced a polished video that confidently showcases my communication skills, demonstrating my readiness for the professional workplace and significantly boosting my confidence for future real-world interviews.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
